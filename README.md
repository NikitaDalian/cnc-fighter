# cnc-fighter
HUMAN vs CNC — Browser fighting game. Defend your factory from the machine uprising. RU/EN/ZH

Your factory has fallen. Five CNC machines — from a rogue furnace to a 200-ton Wuhan Heavy gantry — have declared independence and are refusing to take orders. You are the last operator standing. No IT support, no manual, no mercy. Dodge critical hits, survive rage mode, block when the welder throws a double arc, and pray your combo holds when the machining center starts quoting G-code at you. 
This is HUMAN vs CNC — a browser fighting game where industrial sovereignty is earned one boss at a time, and the night shift is watching.
